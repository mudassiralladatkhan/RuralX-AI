RuralX AI Diagnostic System

Advanced Multimodal Inference Pipeline v2

Triple-Ensemble Architecture

Powered by three optimized DenseNet-121 backbones running concurrently. Predictions are aggregated using softmax averaging for superior accuracy and robustness against noise.

Uncertainty Calibration

Implements Monte Carlo (MC) Dropout across 10 stochastic forward passes. This quantifies epistemic uncertainty, ensuring the model flags unreliable predictions rather than guessing blindly.

XAI Heatmaps

Utilizes Gradient-weighted Class Activation Mapping (Grad-CAM) to generate high-resolution overlay heatmaps, providing visual explainability of radiological findings to clinicians.

Longitudinal Cloud Sync

Fully integrated with Supabase. Automatically tracks patient scan history over time and computes precise mathematical health deltas between current and previous probability distributions.
