About RuralX AI

                    
System Specifications & Medical Capabilities

                

                

                    
Architecture

                    
RuralX AI utilizes a Triple DenseNet-121 Ensemble optimized for local CPU execution. It employs MC Dropout for uncertainty estimation and Laplacian variance for image quality gating.

                    
Clinical Target

                    
Detects Tuberculosis and Pneumonia. Incorporates SpO2 levels and clinical history to adjust triage risk accurately without compromising core model weights.
